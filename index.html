What you get with RigelOS?

Welcome to Team Rigelis, where we are committed to developing the world's leading operating system specifically designed for the last-mile distribution and management of finished pharmaceutical products. Introducing our proprietary operating system, RigelOS™, which is tailored to bring simplicity, efficiency, and precision to retail pharmacy operations.

Experience the Freedom of Rigel OS -Offline First, No Internet Required!

Rigel OS offers you the ultimate convenience - you don't need the internet to make it work! You can deploy it fully offline, and only occasionally connect to the internet for updates, backups, or remote access for managers and owners. Even in the rare event of an internet outage, our in-pharmacy software keeps running smoothly with all its features intact.

Smart Inter Pharmacy Product Transfer.

Experience seamless and efficient inter-pharmacy product transfers with our proprietary Smart Inter-Pharmacy Product Transfer feature. Eliminate concerns about variations in names or units as RigelOS simplifies the process. Initiate the transfer, and let RigelOS handle the rest.

Dedicated customer support team at your service.

We are here to help with any troubleshooting issues you might encounter. We'll make sure you and your staff are well-trained in using the software, and we even offer refresher training if needed.
